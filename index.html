BROOKLYN BRIDGE PARK AUGUST 5TH - 8TH

Limited tickets. Book now to avoid dissapointment!

ABOUT

The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival was created in order to bring the public together and help them discover amazing films. The festival has just been approved to show movies at Brooklyn Bridge Park this year. The festival will run for four consecutive days, from the August 5th to August 8th.

Seats are limited to 5,000 and are filling up fast. We recommend that you book your seat as soon as possible in order to avoid dissappointment. We can't wait to see you there and share with you our love of film!
